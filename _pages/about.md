I am a second-year undergraduate at the [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](https://hkust.edu.hk), pursuing a triple major in **Computer Science, Mathematics, and Artificial Intelligence**. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. [Yangqiu Song](https://www.cse.ust.hk/~yqsong/) at the HKUST [HKUST KnowComp Group](http://hkust-knowcomp.github.io/).

My research interests center on **natural language processing**, with a focus on the **reasoning** and **evaluation** of large language models and vision-language models. I am especially interested in *abductive* and *multimodal* reasoning — how models form, defend, and revise hypotheses under ambiguity. So far I have been first author of papers at **ACL 2026** (Main Conference) and **EMNLP 2025 Workshop** (Spotlight).

I am currently looking for a **research-exchange position in the U.S.** for the upcoming term. If you are a faculty member working on related topics and have an opening, I would be glad to chat — please feel free to reach out via email.
